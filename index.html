SUSHI

SUSHI MAKING

INGREDIENT

  • Rice
  • vinegar
  • sugar
  • salt
  • nori sheet

STEPS TO MAKE

TO MAKE SUSHI
  1. fist prepare the rice
  2. by cooking and seasoning it with vinegar,sugar and salt
  3. next,lay a sheet of nori on a it
  4. and add your fillings like fish or vegetables
  5. roll the sushi tightly using the mat
  6. seal the edge
  7. slice the roll into bitesized pieces
  8. and serve with soy sauce wasabi and pickled ginger

MAIN INGREDIENTS

  • sushi rice
  • nori sheet

DESCRIPTION

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish featuring vinegared rice combined with other ingredients like raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and sometimes egg. Despite its common association with raw fish, the defining component is the specially prepared rice, which is seasoned with a mix of vinegar, sugar, and salt
