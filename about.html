RECIPE DESCRIPTION

What Is Sushi?Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish made from vinegared rice, combined with various ingredients like raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits

Description of sushi Core component: The essential ingredient is cold, cooked rice that has been seasoned with a vinegar mixture, not the seafood. The word "sushi" refers to this vinegared rice. Ingredients: It can be prepared with a wide range of fillings and toppings, including raw or cooked fish (like tuna, salmon, and eel), vegetables (such as cucumber and avocado), and egg. Vinegar mixture: Sushi rice is made with rice vinegar, salt, and sugar, which gives it a unique, slightly sweet and sour flavor and a sticky texture that holds its shape
